Visionary

You have a clear vision about how to address a challenge in terms of analytical strategy, objectives, and necessary skills

You give a one-minute pitch to attract investors and employees who have the right attitude and skills to implement your vision

You hold 100 tokens to compensate team members

Employee

You have interests that resonate with a certain vision, challenge, or strategy, and a unique skillset to contribute

You do quick interviews with visionaries, investors, and other employees to get yourself hired and negotiate compensation

Investor

You are attracted to a particular vision, challenge, or strategy and control the financial resources needed to make it succeed

You also do interviews and negotiate compensation, but in order to decide which team receives your support

You multiply the effectiveness of the team

Rules

  • Form teams of 7-8 people
  • Ensure all skills are covered
  • Skills ranked higher are worth more
  • You can have multiple investors

Scoring

Your team's effectiveness depends on the variety and ranking of employees' skills and determines the team's number of points

But effective teams are useless without funding...
